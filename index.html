SIGMOD 2026 Fellowship

We are pleased to announce the availability of a fellowship for SIGMOD 2026.

This fellowship is intended to support participation in SIGMOD 2026 by researchers and academicians of Indian origin.

Fellowship Coverage

The fellowship will cover a part of the costs associated with attending SIGMOD 2026. Specific details regarding the extent of financial support will be communicated to selected recipients.

Eligibility

  • Participants must be researchers or academicians
  • Applicants must be of Indian origin
  • Applicants should be attending SIGMOD 2026

Deadlines

Application deadlines will be announced soon.
